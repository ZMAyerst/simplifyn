What is Simplifyn?

Simplifyn started out as any company does, An Idea. While redocorating her home, Katie thought that there had to be an easier way to do this. Thus the idea of Simplifyn started. Katie wanted to create a website that took into consideration the space in your home, and matched you with the style and size that most suits your needs. Simplifyn does just that

How does it work?

The first step is to sign up and confirm your account.

Next you fill out all the information about the room you'd like to Spice Up. Theres also an option to add multiple rooms. This information includes The color Scheme, Size, and, Style of the room you're wanting to decorate. Then we do the hard part, all within a click of a button
